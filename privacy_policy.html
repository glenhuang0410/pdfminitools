Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

PDF Mini Tools ("the App") is developed by PDF Mini Tools ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

PDF Mini Tools is designed to work entirely offline. All documents, images, signatures, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external server.

2. Data Storage

All data created or processed by the App (including scanned documents, merged PDFs, signatures, and warranty records) is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. We have no access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing – for processing in-app purchases (premium upgrade and optional donations). Google may collect payment-related information according to their own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • Google ML Kit (on-device) – for text recognition during document scanning. All ML Kit processing occurs entirely on your device; no data is sent to Google's servers.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Google Play.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our target audience is 18 years and older.

6. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera – to scan documents
  • Storage/Files – to read and save PDF files
  • Billing – to process in-app purchases

These permissions are used solely for their stated purposes and no data is transmitted externally.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. No analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are integrated into the App.

8. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, its security depends on your device's security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secured.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
